## 🌐 ENGLISH VERSION (for English App Store / international)

### Subtitle
Tulip Fields · Bloom · Routes

### Description

Find the most beautiful tulip fields in the Netherlands — always with live bloom status.

TulipDay is your personal guide to the Dutch flower season. Whether you're looking for a spectacular field of colour, a relaxed cycling route through the Bollenstreek, or a family day out — TulipDay gets you to the right place at the right time.

**WHAT CAN TULIPDAY DO?**

🌸 Live bloom status
See whether flowers are in full bloom, almost open, or already fading. Updated weekly during the season.

📍 Discover locations near you
Use 'Near me' to find tulip fields, photo spots, and attractions nearby — sorted by distance.

🗺 Ready-made routes
Follow mapped-out routes by car, bike, or on foot. With stops at the most beautiful fields along the way.

📷 Top photo spots
Curated locations for photographers, with tips on the best time and angle.

🔍 Search & filter
Filter by bloom type, distance, or category. Search directly by name or location.

❤️ Save your favourites
Save locations and routes to your own list — no account needed, stored on your device.

**NO ACCOUNT REQUIRED**
TulipDay works completely anonymously. No registration, no email address. Your data stays on your device.

Season: March – May · The Netherlands
